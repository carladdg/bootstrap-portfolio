About Me

Hello and welcome to (the beginnings of) my professional portfolio page! I’m Carla: a clumsy, curious, curly-haired coding enthusiast who’s crazy for comedy flicks and chocolate. I also tend to overuse alliteration.

I was born and raised in the sunny Manila, Philippines, which I will always call home. I lived there for the first 18 years of my life before moving to Evanston, IL to complete my undergraduate studies at Northwestern University. I graduated in June 2017 with a degree in journalism, a second major in psychology and a certificate in integrated marketing communications. Now, I am working in strategy at a Chicago-based advertising agency.

Never one to stop learning, however, I decided to enroll in a coding boot camp to develop new skills that I can leverage both within my field and beyond! Check out my projects on this site.
